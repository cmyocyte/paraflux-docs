# Options Trading (Roadmap)

> **This product is on the roadmap and not yet available.** Options trading will be introduced in a future release.

For the planned design, see [Options](../protocol/options.md).

For option-like exposure available today, see [Power Perpetuals](../protocol/power-perps.md) -- power perps provide convex exposure similar to a perpetual ATM straddle, without strikes, expiry, or liquidity fragmentation.
